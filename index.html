ABOUT DOYIN

Azeezat was a very good and jovial friend. She cared so much about others. She was humble and didn't feel like she was better or experienced than others; she was ready to learn from others. I sure miss her and the memories we shared back at MASS tutorial. Though the time spent was a bit short, it is one I won't forget. Rest on, sis. We love you. Doyin was a rare and unique person. Once you meet her, you never forget her. Adedoyin aka Vibe queen. Doyin was completely loyal to her friends. She had a heart that cared completely. She’s one of those gems everyone needs in their corner! Very real and smart. A few will remember her as a scholar When it came to studying just leave it for Doyin cause she never got tired of studying, she wanted to become a Doctor so bad. To Doyin it was all about achieving goals and making a better world for her family and friends. She loved seeing everybody happy but still struggled to make her self happy. She was Rihanna’s biggest fan and Kizz Daniel’s side chick, she never for once got tired of posting them. When it’s comes to fine locations, leave it for Doyin. She enjoyed going out and taking cute pictures ones she would end up not posting. She never for once backstabbed her friends. She was indeed a good friend.
